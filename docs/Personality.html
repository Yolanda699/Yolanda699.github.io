Psychological Traits [Box Plot Analysis]

How does personality affect the drug consumption patterns?

This page will present a box plot analysis examining the influence of scores obtained in five personality factors from the NEO-FFI-R questionnaire on patterns of drug consumption. The analyzed factors include Neuroticism (N Score), Extraversion (E Score), Openness to Experience (O Score), Agreeableness (A Score), and Conscientiousness (C Score). A higher score indicates a greater likelihood of experiencing specific feelings within each respective category.
